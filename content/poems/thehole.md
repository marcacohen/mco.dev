Once I got distracted
By the neon lights
And I fell into a
Deep, dark hole.

No matter how hard I tried
I couldn’t climb out
Even now
I’m still trying.

When you fall
Hard and fast and far
It changes you
It changes you forever.

> -- <cite>Marc Cohen</cite>
