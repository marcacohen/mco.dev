> Morning quietly arrives  
> A bird sings us awake  
> Yesterday recedes  
> And a new day begins  
>
> Summer in Seattle  
> One little angel  
> Floats into the room  
> Incandescent smile shining  
> As bright as the Sun  
>  
> -- <cite>Marc Cohen</cite>
