Once I got distracted  
By the neon lights  
And I fell into a  
Deep, dark hole.  
<!--more-->

No matter how hard I tried  
I couldn’t climb out  
Even now  
I’m still trying.  

When you fall  
Hard and fast  
It changes you  
It changes you forever.  

--<cite>Marc Cohen</cite>
